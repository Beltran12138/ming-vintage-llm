What you're seeing: ten side-by-side response pairs from a 100-probe battery. ft is Qwen 2.5 3B with the ming-vintage LoRA adapter loaded (trained on 460M characters of pre-1424 文言 from kanripo). bl is the same base model without the adapter. Same prompt, same temperature (0.7), same seed budget.

What to look for: not "who is right" — both responses are generated, both are stochastic, neither is authoritative. Look at which question-space the model lands in. The adapter shifts vocabulary, register, and causal structure. Sometimes it preserves content in classical voice (Q5 蒸汽機). Sometimes it rejects the question entirely and falls into the nearest classical attractor (Q9 互聯網 → 「天工之浩瀚」). Sometimes it fabricates (Q8 哥倫布 → 「翰林學士」). Sometimes it leaks the base model's 2024 priors despite explicit refusal (Q6 二零二一年).
